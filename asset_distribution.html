Asset Distribution

I simulated the model for 200 periods 10000 times. I set initial human capital to 1 and assets to 0 for all agents, reflecting being born with no assets and the replacement level of human capital. I interpolated the calculated policy functions and used random draws to simulate level of human capital and assets for each agent at each point in time.
